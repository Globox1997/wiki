## Description
A rare candy is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) which will level up the player by one level. It will only grant as much level experience as required to get to the next level so watch out when you use it. The idea is from the game [Pokémon](https://www.pokemon.com/). 

Random fact: The creator of this mod used to play the [blue edition](https://www.pokemon.com/us/pokemon-video-games/pokemon-red-version-and-pokemon-blue-version) of [Pokémon](https://www.pokemon.com/).


## Obtaining
Rare candies are typically not [craftable](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Crafting) or obtainable in survival mode but when the player reaches level 100, an archivement will grant one precious rare candy.

## Usage
While holding a rare candy in the players hand, just use right click to consume it.
