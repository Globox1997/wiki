## Description
A golden watering can is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) that can hold [water](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Water). It is used to moisture [farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland). If full it can moisture [farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland) 5 times with a moistening area of 9 [blocks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block).

## Usage
The golden watering can can be filled at a [water](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Water) source [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block). To moisture [farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland) just use the can on the [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block).
