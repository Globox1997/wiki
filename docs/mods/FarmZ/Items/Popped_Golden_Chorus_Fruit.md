## Description
Popped golden chorus fruit is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained by [smelting](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smelting) [golden chorus fruit](/wiki/mods/FarmZ/Items/Golden_Chorus_Fruit). Unlike raw [golden chorus fruit](/wiki/mods/FarmZ/Items/Golden_Chorus_Fruit), popped golden chorus fruit is inedible. It has no real use (yet).

## Obtaining
#### Farming
The popped golden chorus fruit is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained by [smelting](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smelting) [golden chorus fruit](/wiki/mods/FarmZ/Items/Golden_Chorus_Fruit).
