## Description
The golden cookie is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) that can be eaten by the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player).

## Obtaining
#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    4=farmz:golden_wheat, Golden Wheat;
    5=farmz:golden_cocoa_beans, Golden Cocoa Beans;
    6=farmz:golden_wheat, Golden Wheat
]
output[farmz:golden_Cookie, Golden Cookie ]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
To eat a golden cookie, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores <span class="icon-element" icon-count="4" icon-id="hunger"></span> hunger and <span class="icon-element" icon-count="1.6" icon-id="saturation"></span> [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).
