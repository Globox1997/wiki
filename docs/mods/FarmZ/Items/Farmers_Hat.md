## Description
The farmers hat is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) which can be worn to cover the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) head. If the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) wears a hat, there is a 5% chance to grow a golden crop instead of a normal variant.

## Usage
A [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) just has to wear the hat to have a chance of growing golden crops.
