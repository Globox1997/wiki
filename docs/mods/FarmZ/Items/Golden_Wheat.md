## Description
Golden wheat are [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from golden wheat blocks, and are used to craft [golden bread](/wiki/mods/FarmZ/Items/Golden_Bread). 

## Obtaining
#### Farming
Golden wheat is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained by breaking golden wheat blocks.
