## Description
The golden beetroot is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) that can be eaten by the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player).

## Obtaining
#### Farming
Golden Beetroots are obtained from harvesting a golden beetroot block, which drops 1 golden beetroot and 1-4 [seeds](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Beetroot_Seeds) (2 5⁄7 [seeds](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Beetroot_Seeds) per [plant](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Plant) harvested on average). [Seed](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Beetroot_Seeds) yield can be increased using a [tool](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tool) enchanted with [Fortune](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fortune), with [Fortune III](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fortune) harvesting an average of 5 3⁄7 [seeds](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Beetroot_Seeds) per [plant](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Plant). 

## Usage
To eat a golden beetroot, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores <span class="icon-element" icon-count="2" icon-id="hunger"></span> hunger and <span class="icon-element" icon-count="4.8" icon-id="saturation"></span> [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).
