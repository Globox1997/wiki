## Description
Glistering sweet berries is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) that can be eaten by the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player).

## Obtaining
#### Harvesting
Golden sweet berries can be collected from a golden sweet berry bush by pressing the use control on it, yielding 1â€“2 golden sweet berries. After dropping the berries on the ground, the golden sweet berry bush reverts to the second growth stage of a [sweet berry bush](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Sweet_Berries). 

## Usage
To eat a glistering sweet berries, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores <span class="icon-element" icon-count="4" icon-id="hunger"></span> hunger and <span class="icon-element" icon-count="1.6" icon-id="saturation"></span> [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).
