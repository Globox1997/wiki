## Description
Golden Cocoa beans are [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from golden cocoa pods, and are used to craft [golden cookies](/wiki/mods/FarmZ/Items/Golden_Cookie). 

## Obtaining
#### Farming
Golden Cocoa Beans is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained by breaking golden cocoa pods.
