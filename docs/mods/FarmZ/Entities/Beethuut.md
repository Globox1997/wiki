## Story

*The Beethuut is a gentle creature known for its cute voice, adding to its charm. What a derp*

---

## Behavior

The Beethuut is passive and will not attack when provoked. When it hangs out on top of a [farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland) with a [crop](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Crop) on it, from time to time it will try to grow the [plant](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Plant).
