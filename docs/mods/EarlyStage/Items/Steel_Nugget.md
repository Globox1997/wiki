## Description
Steel nuggets are small pieces of steel that are used to craft various [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) such as [steel ingots](/wiki/mods/EarlyStage/Items/Steel_Ingot). One steel nugget is effectively worth one-ninth of a [steel ingot](/wiki/mods/EarlyStage/Items/Steel_Ingot).

## Obtaining
<div id="crafting-table">
