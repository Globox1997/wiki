## Description
The steel ingot is a common metal ingot. It can be smelted from an [iron ingot](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ingot) + [coal](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Coal) and used to craft [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) and [tools](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tool).

## Obtaining
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="blast_furnace_smelting">
input[
    1=iron_ingot, Iron Ingot, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ingot;
    2=coal, Coal, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Coal
]
output[earlystage:steel_ingot, Steel Ingot]
</div>
</div>
