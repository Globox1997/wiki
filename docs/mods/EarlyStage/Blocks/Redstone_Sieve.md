## Description
The redstone sieve is the automated version of the [sieve](/wiki/mods/EarlyStage/Blocks/Sieve). It can sift [blocks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) automatically by giving it a [redstone signal](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Redstone_mechanics).

## Usage
Place [blocks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) inside of the redstone sieve and press the [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) through by just right clicking the redstone sieve or give it a [redstone signal](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Redstone_mechanics) to process the [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block).
