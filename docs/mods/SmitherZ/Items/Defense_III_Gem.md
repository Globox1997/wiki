## Description
The defense gem is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to enhance [armor](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Armor). It will increase the protection of the [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) when it is applied on it. A gem can be linked to an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table).

## Obtaining
#### Loot
The defense III gem can be dropped by the following entities with a chance of 0.5%.

- [Evoker](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Evoker)
- [Pillager](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pillager)
- [Ravager](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ravager)
- [Vindicator](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Vindicator)
- [Witch](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Witch)

## Usage
The defense III gem increases the protection of an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) by a value of 6 and can be linked at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table) with a linking chance of 25%. The linking chance of the gem can be increased with a [smither hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Smither_Hammer/), respectively the unlinking chance of the gem can be incrased with an [extraction hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Extraction_Hammer/).

- Drop Rate: 0.5%
- Link Chance: 25%
- Unlink Chance: 20%
- Link Break Chance: 0%
