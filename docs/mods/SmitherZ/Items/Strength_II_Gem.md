## Description
The strength gem is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to enhance [tools](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tool) or [weapons](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Weapon). It will increase the attack strength of the [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) when it is applied on it. A gem can be linked to an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table).

## Obtaining
#### Loot
The strength II gem can be dropped by the following entities with a chance of 0.5%.

- [Spider](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spider)
- [Creeper](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Creeper)
- [Husk](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Husk)
- [Phantom](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Phantom)
- [Zombie](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Zombie)
- [Slime](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Slime)
- [Skeleton](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Skeleton)
- [Cave Spider](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cave_Spider)
- [Zombie Villager](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Zombie_Villager)

## Usage
The strength II gem increases the attack strength of an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) by a value of 4 and can be linked at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table) with a linking chance of 33%. The linking chance of the gem can be increased with a [smither hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Smither_Hammer/), respectively the unlinking chance of the gem can be incrased with an [extraction hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Extraction_Hammer/).

- Drop Rate: 0.5%
- Link Chance: 33%
- Unlink Chance: 30%
- Link Break Chance: 0%
