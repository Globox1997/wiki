## Description
The health gem is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to enhance [armor](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Armor). It will increase the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) maximum health points. A gem can be linked to an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table).

## Obtaining
#### Loot
The health V gem is not obtainable in survival mode.

## Usage
The health V gem increases the maximum health points of the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) by a value of 12 and can be linked at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table) with a linking chance of 1%. The linking chance of the gem can be increased with a [smither hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Smither_Hammer/), respectively the unlinking chance of the gem can be incrased with an [extraction hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Extraction_Hammer/).

- Drop Rate: 0%
- Link Chance: 1%
- Unlink Chance: 1%
- Link Break Chance: 5%
