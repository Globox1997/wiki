## Description
The speed gem is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to enhance [armor](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Armor). It will increase the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) walking speed. A gem can be linked to an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table).

## Obtaining
#### Loot
The speed I gem can be dropped by the following entities with a chance of 1%.

- [Spider](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spider)
- [Creeper](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Creeper)
- [Husk](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Husk)
- [Phantom](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Phantom)
- [Zombie](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Zombie)
- [Slime](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Slime)
- [Skeleton](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Skeleton)
- [Cave Spider](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cave_Spider)
- [Zombie Villager](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Zombie_Villager)

## Usage
The speed I gem increases the walking speed of the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) by a value of 0.05 and can be linked at the [smithing table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smithing_Table) with a linking chance of 45%. The linking chance of the gem can be increased with a [smither hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Smither_Hammer/), respectively the unlinking chance of the gem can be incrased with an [extraction hammer](/wiki/mods/SmitherZ/Items/Extraction_Hammer/).

- Drop Rate: 1%
- Link Chance: 45%
- Unlink Chance: 50%
- Link Break Chance: 0%
