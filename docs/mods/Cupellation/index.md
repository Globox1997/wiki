# Description
This mod adds the smelter. A multi block mainly out of deepslate. The smelter grants the ability to smelt metal. While the normal furnace just grants low grade ingots, the smelter can produce medium or high grade ingots to craft better tools/gear. Slug is a byproduct during the smelt process and can be recycled to the original material by giving the smelter the correct flux item, like calcite or quarts powder. To cast good quality ingots, a certain casting temperature is required.
