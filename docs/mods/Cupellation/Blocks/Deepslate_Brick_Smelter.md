## Description
The deepslate brick smelter is the [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) used to smelt materials like [gold ore](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Gold_Ore) or [iron ore](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ore). It requires [fuel](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smelting#Fuel) to melt items and can melt up to 3 [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the same time. A smelter is a multi block and has to be built with multiple [blocks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block). Find an example [here](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Structures/Smelter). A [redstone signal](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Redstone_mechanics#Redstone_signal) can be sent to the smelter to start or keep burning [fuel](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smelting#Fuel) to reach or maintain the smelters temperature. Depending on the temperature, low, medium, or high grade items can be casted. During the smelting process of items/blocks, the molten material will produce slag partly. Slag is unused and can be turned back into its original material by adding flux into the smelter.

## Usage
Build the whole multiblock [smelter](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Structures/Smelter) to activate the smelter block. If correctly build, you can access the smelter screen by right clicking the smelter block. The smelter requires a minimum temperature to smelt [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item).
