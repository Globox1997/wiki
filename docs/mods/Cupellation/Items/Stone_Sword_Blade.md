## Description
The stone sword blade is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) made out of [cobblestone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cobblestone) and can be crafted into a [sword](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Sword).

## Obtaining
#### Stonecutting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="stonecutting">
input[ 1=cobblestone, Cobblestone, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cobblestone ]
output[cupellation:stone_sword_blade, Stone Sword Blade]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
The stone sword blade is mainly used to cast a [sword blade mold](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Items/Sword_Blade_Mold) by putting it on the [casting table](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Blocks/Deepslate_Casting_Table) and casting material around it.

#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    5=cupellation:stone_sword_blade, Stone Sword Blade;
    8=stick, Stick, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Stick
]
output[stone_sword, Stone Sword ]
</div>
</div>
