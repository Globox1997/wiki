## Description
The diamond pickaxe head is a castable [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the smelter and can be crafted into a [pickaxe](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pickaxe).

## Obtaining
A diamond pickaxe head can only be obtained by putting a [pickaxe head mold](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Items/Pickaxe_Head_Mold) on the [casting table](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Blocks/Deepslate_Casting_Table) and casting material around it.
