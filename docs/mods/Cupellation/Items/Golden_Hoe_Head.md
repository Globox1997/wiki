## Description
The golden hoe head is a castable [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) at the smelter and can be crafted into a [hoe](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hoe).

## Obtaining
A golden hoe head can only be obtained by putting a [hoe head mold](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Items/Hoe_Head_Mold) on the [casting table](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Blocks/Deepslate_Casting_Table) and casting material around it.
