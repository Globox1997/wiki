## Description
The stone pickaxe head is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) made out of [cobblestone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cobblestone) and can be crafted into a [pickaxe](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pickaxe).

## Obtaining
#### Stonecutting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="stonecutting">
input[ 1=cobblestone, Cobblestone, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cobblestone ]
output[cupellation:stone_pickaxe_head, Stone Pickaxe Head]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
The stone pickaxe head is mainly used to cast a [pickaxe head mold](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Items/Pickaxe_Head_Mold) by putting it on the [casting table](/wiki/mods/Cupellation/Blocks/Deepslate_Casting_Table) and casting material around it.

#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    2=cupellation:stone_pickaxe_head, Stone Pickaxe Head;
    5=stick, Stick, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Stick;
    8=stick, Stick, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Stick
]
output[stone_pickaxe, Stone Pickaxe ]
</div>
</div>
