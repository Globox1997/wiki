## Description
Aftermath is a [status effect](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Effect) that makes the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) slower and reduces the [attack damage](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Melee_attack#Attack_damage) during the duration of the [effect](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Effect).

## Effect
The aftermath effect decreases the [walking](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Walking) speed by 30 % and reduces the [attack damage](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Melee_attack#Attack_damage) by 5.
