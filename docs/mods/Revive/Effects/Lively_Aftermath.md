## Description
Lively aftermath is a [status effect](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Effect) that makes the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) slower during the duration of the [effect](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Effect).

## Effect
The lively aftermath effect decreases the [walking](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Walking) speed by 15 %.
