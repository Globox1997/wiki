## Description
The revivify potion is a [consumable drink](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Drinks) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) which will revive a [dead](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Health#Death) [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player). As a side effect, the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) will be given the [aftermath effect](/wiki/mods/Revive/Effects/Aftermath/).

## Usage
To revive a friend, just use right click on the [dead](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Health#Death) [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) body.
