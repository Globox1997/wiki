## Description
The cherry wood rack is an utility [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) used to dry various [items](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item). It is mainly used to process [hide](/wiki/mods/FleshZ/Items/Hide) into [leather](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Leather).

## Obtaining
#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    4=cherry_slab, Cherry Slab, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cherry_Slab;
    5=cherry_slab, Cherry Slab, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cherry_Slab;
    6=cherry_slab, Cherry Slab, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cherry_Slab
]
output[fleshz:cherry_wood_rack, Cherry Wood Rack ]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
If an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) is processable at the wood rack, just put the [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) onto it with right click on the rack. If the wood rack has a hanging [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item), just use right click on the rack again to pull the [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) into your inventory.
