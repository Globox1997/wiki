## Description
Heating stones is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to increase the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) [body temperature](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/).

## Obtaining
#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="stonecutting">
input[
    1=stone, Stone, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Stone
]
output[environmentz:heating_stones, Heating Stones ]
description="Unheated"
</div>
</div>

## Usage
While holding the heated heating stones in the hand, it will increase the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) [body temperature](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/).

#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="furnace_smelting">
input[
    1=damaged_environmentz:heating_stones, Heating Stones
]
output[environmentz:heated_stones, Heating Stones ]
description="Heated"
</div>
</div>
