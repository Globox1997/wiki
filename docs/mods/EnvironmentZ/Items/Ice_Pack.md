## Description
An ice pack is an [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) used to decrease the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) [body temperature](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/).

## Obtaining
#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    2=iron_ingot, Iron Ingot, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ingot;
    4=leather, Leather, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Leather;
    5=ice, Ice, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ice;
    6=leather, Leather, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Leather;
    8=leather, Leather, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Leather
]
output[environmentz:ice_pack, Ice Pack ]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
While holding the ice pack in the hand, it will decrease the [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) [body temperature](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/).
