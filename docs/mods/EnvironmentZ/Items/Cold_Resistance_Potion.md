## Description
The cold resistance potion is a [consumable drink](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Drinks) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) which grants the [warming effect](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Effects/Warming/) which will protect the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) from cold [temperatures](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/).

## Usage
To drink a cold resistance potion, press and hold right click while it is selected in the hotbar.
