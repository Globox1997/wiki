## Description
Cooling is a [status effect](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Effect) that will reduce the [overheating temperature effect](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/) of the environment. It can be removed by drinking [milk](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket). The [cold resistance potion](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Items/Cold_Resistance_Potion/) will grant this status effect.

## Effect
The cooling effect will reduce or even completely eliminate the overheating environment [temperature](/wiki/mods/EnvironmentZ/Mechanic/), keeping the player in a quite cool and well state.
