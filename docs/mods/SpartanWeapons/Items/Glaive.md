## Description
A glaive is a [weapon](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Weapon) that deals [damage](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Damage).

## Obtaining
Depending on the pole variant, the handlebar of the glaive is determined.

#### Crafting
<div id="crafting-table">
<div class="crafting-element" crafting-type="vanilla_crafting">
input[
    2=diamond, Diamond, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Diamond;
    5=diamond, Diamond, https://minecraft.wiki/w/Diamond;
    8=spartanweapons:oak_pole, Oak Pole, SpartanWeapons/Items/Oak_Pole, true
]
output[spartanweapons:diamond_oak_glaive, Glaive]
</div>
</div>

## Usage
Pressing attack while holding a glaive inflicts damage on both [mobs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob) and other [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player). Upon damaging a [mob](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob) or [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player), the glaive's [durability](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Durability) decreases by 1.
