## Description
A staff is a [weapon](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Weapon) that deals [damage](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Damage).

## Obtaining
Depending on the material, the staff variant is determined.

## Usage
Pressing attack while holding a staff inflicts damage on both [mobs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob) and other [players](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player). Upon damaging a [mob](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob) or [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player), the staff's [durability](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Durability) decreases by 1.
