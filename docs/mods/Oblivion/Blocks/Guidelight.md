## Description
The guidelight block is the main [block](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Block) of the [guidelight](/wiki/mods/Oblivion/Structures/Guidelight). When the guidelight is active, an aura of energy will be visible coming from the guidelight block.

## Obtaining
Once teleported to the Oblivion dimension, a [guidelight](/wiki/mods/Oblivion/Structures/Guidelight) will be found on this side of the dimension. In the [Overworld](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Overworld) a [guidelight](/wiki/mods/Oblivion/Structures/Guidelight) has to build by the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player). Only one [guidelight](/wiki/mods/Oblivion/Structures/Guidelight) can be build.
