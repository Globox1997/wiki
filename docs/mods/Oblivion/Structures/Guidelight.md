## Description
The guidelight is a [structure](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Structure) which is used to teleport from and to the Oblivion dimension.

## Generation
The [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) has to build the [structure](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Structure) anywhere in the [Overworld](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Overworld).  
When the [structure](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Structure) is build, it will take sometime to generate enough energy to teleport entities to the Oblivion dimension, indicated by an aura of energy surrouinding the [structure](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Structure). Only one working guidelight can be build in the [Overworld](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Overworld) and in the Oblivion dimension a guidelight will await.

#### Blocks
- 36 Stone Bricks
- 1 Guidelight
